## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: What's included in commercial support?**
A: Email support, priority bug fixes, technical documentation, and 48-hour response time.

**Q: How do I upgrade to enterprise support?**
A: Contact enterprise@ddsp-piano.com to discuss your needs and receive a custom proposal.

**Q: Can I get support for open-source use?**
A: Yes, through GitHub Issues and community discussions. Commercial support requires a license.

**Q: What's the difference between commercial and enterprise support?**
A: Enterprise includes SLA-backed support, dedicated account manager, custom development, and faster response times.

### Technical Questions

**Q: How do I report a bug?**
A: Use GitHub Issues for open-source, or email support@ddsp-piano.com with your license key for commercial support.

**Q: Can you help with integration?**
A: Yes, integration assistance is available with commercial and enterprise support. Enterprise customers can get dedicated integration services.

**Q: Do you provide custom model training?**
A: Yes, custom model training is available with enterprise support. Contact enterprise@ddsp-piano.com for details.

**Q: Can you help with deployment?**
A: Yes, deployment assistance is included in all support tiers. Enterprise customers get dedicated deployment support.

### Billing Questions

**Q: How is support billed?**
A: Commercial support is monthly subscription. Enterprise support is custom pricing based on requirements.

**Q: Can I cancel support?**
A: Yes, commercial support can be cancelled monthly. Enterprise support terms are in your agreement.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**
A: Support subscriptions are non-refundable, but we work to ensure satisfaction. Contact us to discuss.
